About Us

Who are we?

The F2M concept arose from an unforeseen event: the owner and CEO of the farm to market began a car buying and selling business, but when the pandemic struck in 2020, this enterprise failed since no one wanted to avail. So, the man behind it decided to acquire and start a piggery business, putting all of his funds into it, and it was first shaky and difficult.
